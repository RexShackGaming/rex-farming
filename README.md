# rex-farming

A comprehensive farming system for RedM that allows players to plant, grow, and harvest crops with a dynamic growth system including thirst, hunger, and quality mechanics.

**Game:** RedM (RDR3)  
**Framework:** RSG Framework (ONLY)

## ✨ Key Features

- **Dynamic Plant Growth System** - Plants grow over time with multiple growth stages
- **Thirst & Hunger Mechanics** - Plants need water and fertilizer to thrive
- **Quality System** - Plant quality affects harvest yields
- **Weather Integration** - Rain naturally waters your crops
- **Multiple Crop Types** - Corn, sugarcane, cotton, carrots, potatoes, tomatoes, wheat, and more
- **Water Bucket System** - Fill buckets from water sources to water your plants
  - Fill from water props (wells, troughs, pumps)
  - Fill from natural water sources (rivers, lakes, ponds)
  - 5 uses per bucket with visual feedback
  - Animated filling with gender-appropriate animations
- **Fertilizer Collection** - Collect manure to boost plant growth
- **Plant Ownership** - Only plant owners can harvest their crops
- **Zone-based Planting** - Optional restriction to designated farming areas
- **Anti-Spam Protection** - Cooldowns prevent exploitation
- **Full Localization Support** - Easy translation to any language

## 🆕 Recent Updates

### Natural Water Source Filling
- Players can now fill their water buckets by entering any natural water source
- Stand in rivers, lakes, or ponds with an empty bucket
- Press `E` to fill your bucket with an immersive animation
- Works alongside traditional water props (wells, troughs)

### Enhanced Water Bucket System
- Water buckets now show remaining uses after each watering action
- Visual notifications: "Plant Watered - X uses remaining"
- Must fully empty bucket (0 uses) before refilling
- Prevents watering plants with an empty bucket
